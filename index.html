What is a Bigtop?

Apache Bigtop is setting the standard for testing, packaging and integration of leading Big Data components.

Working with big data components like HDFS, Spark, Hive and Ignite? Looking for ready to use packages and deployment tooling? If so Bigtop is for you.



What does Bigtop provide?

What Bigtop Gives Your Big Data Stack

No matter what your big data infrastructure needs are, Bigtop and its community provides a range of tooling to meet your needs

Packaging

Out of box Bigtop components can provide you with your own Debs and RPM packages

Testing

Gradle powered, integrated test suite for packages, platform, and component integration.

Configuration/Deployment

Out-of-box Puppet Modules supporting the packages and best practices for stack configuration. Ongoing deployment and orchestration in the community

Need Multi OS and Architecture Support?

Bigtop and its community are working to support the widest range of Operating Systems and Chip Architectures
